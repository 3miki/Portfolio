About me

I am a London-based Computer Science graduate with an MSc, transitioning from a diverse background in sales and marketing to follow my passion for technology. My experience in the travel and marketing industies and working with diverse business products has shaped my focus on creating efficient, user-centred software solutions.

Beyond tech, I’m inspired by nature and enjoy hiking, birdwatching, and exploring plant-based cooking. I’m committed to integrating environmental sustainability into my work and supporting eco-friendly initiatives.

I am excited to connect with those who share similar interests and are working on meaningful and impactful challenges. Feel free to reach out for collaboration or to discuss any projects.
